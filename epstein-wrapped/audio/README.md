# Background Music Setup

The Epstein Wrapped experience uses **ironically upbeat music** for dark comedic effect.

## Recommended Tracks (Royalty-Free)

Download one of these tracks and save as `happy-ukulele.mp3`:

### YouTube Audio Library (Free)
1. **"Ukulele"** by BENSOUND - Happy, carefree ukulele
2. **"Happy"** by MBB - Cheerful and upbeat
3. **"Carefree"** by Kevin MacLeod - Light and breezy
4. **"Bright Wish"** by Purrple Cat - Cute, happy vibes

### Pixabay Music (Free)
- Search "happy ukulele" or "cheerful" for ironic contrast

### How to Download from YouTube Audio Library:
1. Go to https://studio.youtube.com/channel/UC/music
2. Search "ukulele" or "happy"
3. Click the download button
4. Rename to `happy-ukulele.mp3`
5. Place in this folder

## The Joke

The music should be:
- Extremely happy/cheerful
- Kids show vibes
- Elevator music energy
- Maximum ironic contrast with dark content

Think: The kind of music that would play in a children's educational video, but over stats about trafficking and "suicide".
