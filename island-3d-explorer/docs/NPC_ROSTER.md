# NPC Roster & Dialogue

## Overview

The NPCs in this experience represent composite characters inspired by real testimony, depositions, and investigative journalism. They are not meant to represent any specific individual.

---

## The Groundskeeper

**ID:** `groundskeeper`
**Location:** Western grounds (-30, 50)
**Color:** Forest Green (#4a7c59)

### Background
A maintenance worker who spent years tending the island's grounds. Witnessed patterns but was bound by NDAs and fear.

### Dialogue Sequence

1. **Initial Contact:**
   > "You're not supposed to be here, you know."

2. **Opening Up:**
   > "I worked the grounds for three years. Saw things... strange things."

3. **Temple Reference:**
   > "The temple? Off limits to most staff. Only certain people went up there."

4. **Traffic Patterns:**
   > "Private jets coming in at all hours. Boats in the middle of the night."

5. **Breaking Silence:**
   > "I signed an NDA. But what good is silence when you can't sleep at night?"

6. **Warning:**
   > "Look around if you must. But be careful what you find."

### Investigation Value
- Confirms restricted areas
- References to unusual schedules
- Mentions staff restrictions

## The Journalist

**ID:** `journalist`
**Location:** Eastern area near pool (100, -50)
**Color:** Dark Blue (#2c3e50)

### Background
An investigative reporter who has been tracking flight logs and following the money. Represents the journalists who broke the story.

### Dialogue Sequence

1. **Recognition:**
   > "Finally, someone else digging into this."

2. **Flight Logs:**
   > "I've been tracking flight logs for months. The patterns are... disturbing."

3. **Power:**
   > "Powerful people. Very powerful. Some names would shock you."

4. **Security:**
   > "The security here was intense. Cameras everywhere, except certain rooms."

5. **Lead:**
   > "Check the staff quarters. There's evidence of a surveillance system."

6. **Warning:**
   > "Be careful. People who ask too many questions tend to have accidents."

### Investigation Value
- Points to staff quarters
- Confirms surveillance gaps
- References to powerful figures

## The Anonymous Witness

**ID:** `witness`
**Location:** Near temple, western ridge (-150, -40)
**Color:** Gray (#7f8c8d)

### Background
A survivor. Their testimony represents the victims who came forward. This character is handled with the gravity it deserves.

### Dialogue Sequence

1. **Anonymity:**
   > "I can't tell you my name. Please understand."

2. **Past:**
   > "I was brought here when I was young. Too young."

3. **The Temple:**
   > "The temple... it wasn't for worship. Not any god I know."

4. **Others:**
   > "There were others. Many others. Some I never saw leave."

5. **Trauma:**
   > "The blue and white stripes - I see them in my nightmares still."

6. **Purpose:**
   > "Document everything. The world needs to know what happened here."

### Investigation Value
- First-person account of temple
- Confirms multiple victims
- Provides emotional weight to investigation

## The Security Guard

**ID:** `security`
**Location:** Security station, eastern edge (190, 10)
**Color:** Dark Gray (#34495e)

### Background
Former private security personnel who was paid for discretion. Has hidden information on the island.

### Dialogue Sequence

1. **Challenge:**
   > "State your business."

2. **Resignation:**
   > "...Fine. I don't work for them anymore anyway."

3. **The Deal:**
   > "We were paid well to see nothing. Hear nothing."

4. **No Records:**
   > "Guest lists were burned after every visit. No records."

5. **Hidden Evidence:**
   > "But I kept notes. Hidden them somewhere on the island."

6. **Location Hint:**
   > "Near the sundial. Under the third stone. If you're brave enough."

### Investigation Value
- **CRITICAL**: Points directly to evidence location
- Confirms document destruction
- Reveals internal record-keeping

## The Dock Worker

**ID:** `dockworker`
**Location:** Eastern beach area (220, 80)
**Color:** Brown (#8b4513)

### Background
Handled cargo and boat arrivals. Saw faces but wasn't supposed to remember them.

### Dialogue Sequence

1. **Odd Hours:**
   > "The boats came at odd hours. Real odd."

2. **Vessels:**
   > "Luxury yachts, mostly. Sometimes cargo containers."

3. **Cargo:**
   > "What was in them? We weren't allowed to ask."

4. **Famous Faces:**
   > "Saw some faces though. Famous faces. Politicians. Actors."

5. **Routine:**
   > "They'd go straight to the main house. Never stayed at the dock long."

6. **False Manifests:**
   > "The manifests were always 'supplies.' Every single time. Just 'supplies.'"

### Investigation Value
- Confirms VIP arrivals
- Suspicious cargo manifests
- Arrival patterns and procedures

## NPC Behavior

### Visual Indicators
- All NPCs have a **green indicator sphere** floating above their heads
- The sphere bobs up and down to catch attention
- Color changes could indicate if they've been spoken to (future feature)

### Interaction
- NPCs face the player when nearby
- Dialogue advances with SPACE or ENTER
- Full dialogue reveals most important information last

### Design Philosophy
NPCs provide:
1. **Atmosphere** - Make the island feel inhabited
2. **Information** - Direct hints to evidence locations
3. **Emotional weight** - Remind players this was real
4. **Pacing** - Break up exploration with narrative moments
